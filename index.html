PawPulse is an opensource community focused on developing non-invasive health monitoring systems for pets

Motivation

As a community of animal lovers we aim to help increase overall understanding of pet wellbeing and grant owners the ability to monitor their pet's health through opensource designs.

Current Project: Steps & Heartrate Monitor

Approach

Our approach is to develop projects for educational purposes rather than profitability. All our designs are opensource which allows the community to actively participate in the development process.
